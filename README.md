# NutApp - Nutrition Management System

Transform your nutritional guidance into actionable meal planning. NutApp enables you to digitize dietary recommendations from your nutritionist and convert them into an organized shopping list. Take control of your nutrition with simplified diet tracking and intelligent inventory management.

**Key Benefits:**

- Convert paper meal plans into digital format
- Generate automated shopping lists based on prescribed meals
- Track dietary adherence and nutritional goals
- Streamline meal preparation planning
